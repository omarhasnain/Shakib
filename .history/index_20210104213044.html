Shakib Al Hasan

Worldclass Allrounder

Shakib Al Hasan is a Bangladeshi international cricketer who was ICC's no.1 all rounder in all three formats of the game in 2016.He is the only captain to take a five-for in T20Is

  • Born: March 24, 1987 (age 33 years), Magura District
  • Test debut (cap 46): 18 May 2007 v India
  • Last Test: 5 September 2019 v Afghanistan
  • ODI debut (cap 82): 6 August 2006 v Zimbabwe
  • Last ODI: 5 July 2019 v Pakistan
  • T20I debut (cap 11): 28 November 2006 v Zimbabwe
